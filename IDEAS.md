# Ideas

## Travel Pulse Insurance and Assistance Partnerships

Package Travel Pulse for insurers, travel apps, assistance providers, and concierge teams as a penny-priced disruption and emergency-awareness check. Their AI, app, or workflow can call the endpoint before departure and during the covered trip. Position it as an awareness feature, not staffed rescue, emergency dispatch, insurance coverage, or official guidance. Any partner packaging must preserve the disclaimer: informational only, may be incomplete/delayed/incorrect, verify with official sources, and contact local emergency services in an emergency.
